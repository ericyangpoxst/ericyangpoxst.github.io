And now Chinese blocks are cheap and of good quality, you can rest assured that you are sourcing blocks from China.

I think, compared to LEGO, the advantage of the Chinese production of blocks is cost-effective, from playability, ornamental and other perspectives can compete with LEGO!

And the Forbidden City national trend, the wandering earth, space culture and creativity and so on well-known domestic IP are through the various Chinese block manufacturers to achieve the blocks, cultural advantages are also very obvious!

However, we also need to see the shortcomings of Chinese building blocks. At present, the quality of the product does not fully achieve a high level of quality of all, most of the uneven, there are high quality at the same time there are also low-quality situation.In addition, the creative design also needs to be strengthened in order to catch up with LEGO!

Chinese building block brand, need time, we also need to give him a chance to develop.

3. Are there still problems with Chinese blocks copying and infringing LEGO?

LEGO’s block patent has actually expired a long time ago, so all building blocks manufacturers can legally use block design.

Today LEGO is relying on the creative design of blocks and the quality of its own blocks to ensure the position of the leading brand brother. I believe that the Chinese made blocks brand will be able to catch up, and there is indeed this trend!
