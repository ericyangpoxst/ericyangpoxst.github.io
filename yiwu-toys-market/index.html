What inside of Yiwu Toys Wholesale Market?

This toy market has four districts, they are district B, district C, district D, and district E.  Every district has 12 streets, so a total of more than 2,000 stores. Each district sales different toys type.

District B have plush toys;

District C have plush toys, inflating toys, party toys, plastic toys;

District D have plastic toys, electric toys, wooden toys;

District E have plastic toys, regular toys, gift toys, party toys;
